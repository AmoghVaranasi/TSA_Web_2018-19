The complete guide to US Citizenship

Citizenship is the common thread that connects all Americans. We are a nation bound not by race or religion, but by the shared values of freedom, liberty, and equality.

A lot of people want to become US citizens. But the "how", "why", and "what" is unknown to some.
This guide includes all of those components, and some more useful information on what a citizen should do, and what your rights are when and if you become a citizen. Thanks for reading this guide.
